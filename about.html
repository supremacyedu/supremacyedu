About Supremacy Edu

Supremacy was founded in 2011 with a concept to provide objective-centric coaching education in a different, innovative and affable learning environment in the challenging field of Boards and Competitive examinations such as IIT-JEE (Main & Advanced), MHT-CET, NEET, AIIMS, and JIPMER, CA Foundation, CA Intermediate, Management & Foundations.

Over Fifteen years of its inception, the institute has risen beyond expectations. The volume and quality of results have proven the success of its mission, with matchless growth in student enrollments and selections in Engineering, Medical, Accountancy & Management Colleges.

Our Strength & Vision

Excellent course material, experienced faculty, and a committed management team have built Supremacy Edu into one of the most trusted institutions in the region.

Our focus remains on delivering academic excellence, structured preparation, and the highest level of student satisfaction while preparing learners for all major examinations.
